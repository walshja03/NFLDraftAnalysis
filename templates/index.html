Prediction Model Summary

The purpose of this project is to try to project the success of this years wide receiver draft class which was historically deep. As an Eagles fan I was skeptical of their first round pick when Justin Jefferson was still on the board. Based on the data I choose to incorporate in this model which included most College stats gathered, class, a few select combine stats based on the analysis that can be seen under the research charts, and pickNum the Eagles made the right pick.

Check out some of the Reseach Charts which I utilized to decide what statistics to use in this model and click the create a model link if you would like to try your hands at choosing a variety of variables and running your own model. I warn you running different models can be addictive and given there are 61 different variables 90,177,170,226 combinations of 10 alone. That being said I intend to expand this by getting more data pertaining to injuries and hope to expand my research to Drafting running backs,tight ends, and maybe eventually QB's.
